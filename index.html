Platform capabilities

Sub-100ms execution

Signals are parsed and orders dispatched to your MT5 account within milliseconds of the Telegram message. Cloud hosted — always on, never misses a signal.

Full trade lifecycle management

Automatically handles signal updates — SL moves, break-even commands, partial closes, TP modifications, and full CLOSE ALL signals. Tracks open and closed trades in your dashboard.

Advanced layering

When a signal contains a price range, layering splits your entry across 2–5 orders at evenly spaced prices. Spreads risk and improves average entry. Configurable per group.

Smart symbol mapping

Providers say "GOLD" — your broker calls it "XAUUSD" or "XAUUSDm.raw". MarketSync automatically maps signal instrument names to your broker's exact symbols on connection.

Deep account analytics

Safe unsubscribe logic

When you unsubscribe from a group, your open trades keep receiving SL moves, TP updates, and close commands until they close naturally — preventing abandoned trades.

Intelligent signal parser

Understands every signal format

MarketSync reads raw Telegram messages and automatically identifies the direction, entry, stop loss, and take profit — even across providers who format signals differently.

  • New signals

    BUY/SELL with entry price, SL and up to 4 TP levels. Market orders when no price is specified.

  • Trade updates

    MOVE SL, RISK FREE AT, CLOSE ALL — parsed from reply messages and applied to all open orders from the original signal.

  • Cancellations and noise

    CANCEL commands and non-signal messages are correctly identified and ignored — no spurious trades.

FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Does MarketSync work with MT4?
No. MarketSync supports MetaTrader 5 (MT5) only. If your broker offers both, ensure you are using your MT5 account credentials when connecting.
Do I need to keep my PC on?
No. MarketSync is entirely cloud-based. Signals are monitored and orders dispatched 24/7 from our servers — your computer and MT5 terminal do not need to be open.
What happens if my payment fails?
You get a 3-day grace period — signal copying continues normally. After 3 days without payment, copying stops but your dashboard remains accessible in read-only mode. Open trades continue receiving SL/TP/close updates until they close naturally.
Can I use any Telegram signal group?
You can subscribe to any of the groups available on the MarketSync platform. These are groups that our Telegram listener accounts are already connected to. New group requests can be submitted to the team.
Is my MT5 password safe?
You provide your MT5 trading password (not the read-only investor password). Credentials are encrypted and transmitted securely. MarketSync uses the industry-standard TradeSync infrastructure to manage MT5 connections.
Does MarketSync provide trading signals?
No. MarketSync is an order routing tool — infrastructure, not advice. You connect to third-party Telegram signal providers entirely at your own discretion. We do not provide signals, analysis, or financial advice.
How does the referral program work?
Every paying subscriber gets a unique referral link. When someone signs up and pays through your link, you earn 10% recurring commission (~$5/month per active referral) for as long as they stay subscribed. Payouts are handled automatically via Rewardful.
What is layering and should I use it?
Layering splits a range-entry signal (e.g. BUY @ 3050/3040) into 2–5 separate orders at evenly spaced prices across the range. This can improve your average entry price. It only activates on signals that include a price range — single-price signals are unaffected.
